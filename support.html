Advent Scholars Foundation

Support LifePoint Through ASF

Advent Scholars Foundation raises funds to support LifePoint Academy. The foundation reviews LifePoint Academy grant requests and funds projects that:

  • Provide tuition support for students
  • Fund enhanced programs in the arts and sciences
  • Help fund projects that improve facilities and accommodate increased enrollment
  • Facilitate spiritual development of students as young disciples of Jesus
