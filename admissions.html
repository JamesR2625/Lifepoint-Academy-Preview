How to Apply

Application Process

Follow the steps below to complete the application process. All student applications will be reviewed by the Admissions Committee for acceptance.

1

Complete the Online Application

Follow the steps in our online application portal to begin your enrollment process. You'll provide student information, family details, and previous school records.

Start Application
2

Submit Your Checklist

Complete and submit the required documentation checklist, including immunization records, birth certificate, and previous report cards.

View Checklist
3

Complete Recommendation Form

Have a teacher or school administrator complete the recommendation form on behalf of your student.

Recommendation Form

We shall process your admission and update you with our final decision within 60 days of receiving payment and all documentation.
