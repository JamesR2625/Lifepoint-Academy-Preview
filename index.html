Welcome to Our School

Shaping Tomorrow's Leaders Today

LifePoint Academy of Seventh-day Adventists is a Pre-K through 10th grade Christian school accredited by the Florida Association of Academic Nonpublic Schools (FAANS), the National Council for Private School Accreditation (NCPSA), and holds the VPK Gold Seal.

We provide a spiritually oriented education that develops the spiritual, mental, and physical powers of each student, preparing them for a life of purpose and service.
