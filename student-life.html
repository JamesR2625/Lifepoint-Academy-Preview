Your Child's Safety

School Security

The LifePoint Academy campus is monitored with security cameras through a security company. Visitors and parents are expected to sign in and wear a name tag at all times. They are allowed into the classroom area only when being accompanied by a teacher or staff member.

Any person interacting with students is required to go through an extensive background check. Students are supervised at all times in class, on the playground, at lunch, and in aftercare.

LifePoint Academy encourages frequent hand washing and has sanitizing stations in each classroom and in other areas of the school. The school is regularly cleaned by a contracted cleaning service.
